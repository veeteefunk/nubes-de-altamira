nubes de Altamira is the musical project of Victor Marin Viloria. Born in Caracas, Venezuela, Victor chose the genre of ambient to express his nostalgia about places, times and feelings associated to his hometown. nubes de Altamira attempts to be introspective, reflective music. It aims at staying in the background, sustaining emotions generated by remembrances.
